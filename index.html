Tips for Success

  • Prioritize Understanding over Speed: If a chapter is complex, do not rush. Use buffer days for difficult topics.
  • CAFM is Key: Treat the CAFM slot as non-negotiable. Spend at least 2.5 hours daily on numerical problems.
  • Active Recall: In the morning, try to write down key points from the previous day's study without looking at notes.
  • Practice Daily: The evening slot for MCQs/Case Studies is vital for applying knowledge and building confidence.
